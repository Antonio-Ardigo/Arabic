# 1. شلونك (shlonak) — "how are you? (to a man)"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | شلونك |
| Transliteration | shlonak |
| English | how are you? (to a man) |
| Forvo | [شلونك](https://forvo.com/word/شلونك/#ar) |
| Local audio | [shlonak.mp4](../audio/shlonak.mp4) |

Literally "what-color-your-state" — Gulf/Najdi greeting. To a woman: `shlonich`.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** شلونك اليوم؟
**Transliteration:** shlonak al-yoom?
**English:** How are you today?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| شلونك | shlonak | how-are-you (m.) |
| اليوم | al-yoom | today |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** شلونك يا خوي؟
**Transliteration:** shlonak ya khooy?
**English:** How are you, my brother?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| شلونك | shlonak | how-are-you (m.) |
| يا | ya | (vocative particle) |
| خوي | khooy | my-brother |
