# 3. وش (wesh) — "what?"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | وش |
| Transliteration | wesh |
| English | what? (Saudi) |
| Forvo | [وش](https://forvo.com/word/وش/#ar) |
| Local audio | [wesh.mp4](../audio/wesh.mp4) |

Saudi/Najdi interrogative. MSA is ماذا / ما — `wesh` is universal in Saudi speech.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** وش تبغى؟
**Transliteration:** wesh tibgha?
**English:** What do you want?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| وش | wesh | what |
| تبغى | tibgha | you-want (m.) |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** وش صار؟
**Transliteration:** wesh saar?
**English:** What happened?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| وش | wesh | what |
| صار | saar | happened |
