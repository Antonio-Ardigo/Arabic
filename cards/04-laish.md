# 4. ليش (laish) — "why?"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | ليش |
| Transliteration | laish |
| English | why? |
| Forvo | [ليش](https://forvo.com/word/ليش/#ar) |
| Local audio | [laish.mp4](../audio/laish.mp4) |

Gulf/Saudi "why". MSA لماذا.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** ليش ما جيت؟
**Transliteration:** laish ma jeet?
**English:** Why didn't you come?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| ليش | laish | why |
| ما | ma | not |
| جيت | jeet | you-came (m.) |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** ليش كذا؟
**Transliteration:** laish kidha?
**English:** Why like this?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| ليش | laish | why |
| كذا | kidha | like-this |
