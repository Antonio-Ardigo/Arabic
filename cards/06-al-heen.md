# 6. الحين (al-heen) — "now"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | الحين |
| Transliteration | al-heen |
| English | now (Najdi; MSA is الآن) |
| Forvo | [الحين](https://forvo.com/word/الحين/#ar) |
| Local audio | [al-heen.mp4](../audio/al-heen.mp4) |

Default Najdi "now". Also heard as `hassa` in other Saudi regions — but `al-heen` is the Najdi form.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** الحين أنا قاعد آكل
**Transliteration:** al-heen ana gaa'id aakol
**English:** Right now I'm eating.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| الحين | al-heen | now |
| أنا | ana | I |
| قاعد | gaa'id | currently (m.) |
| آكل | aakol | I-eat |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** تعال الحين
**Transliteration:** ta'aal al-heen
**English:** Come now.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| تعال | ta'aal | come (imp. m.) |
| الحين | al-heen | now |
