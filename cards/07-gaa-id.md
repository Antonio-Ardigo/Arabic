# 7. قاعد (gaa'id) — "currently (progressive marker)"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | قاعد |
| Transliteration | gaa'id |
| English | currently / -ing (lit. "sitting") |
| Forvo | [قاعد](https://forvo.com/word/قاعد/#ar) |
| Local audio | [gaa-id.mp4](../audio/gaa-id.mp4) |

Najdi progressive aspect: put `gaa'id` before a verb to mean "I'm ___ing right now". Agrees with subject: `gaa'id` (m.), `gaa'da` (f.), `gaa'diin` (pl.). Najdi ق → `g`.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** قاعد أشتغل
**Transliteration:** gaa'id ashtaghil
**English:** I'm working (right now).

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| قاعد | gaa'id | currently (m.) |
| أشتغل | ashtaghil | I-work |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** وش قاعد تسوي؟
**Transliteration:** wesh gaa'id tsawwi?
**English:** What are you doing?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| وش | wesh | what |
| قاعد | gaa'id | currently (m.) |
| تسوي | tsawwi | you-do (m.) |
