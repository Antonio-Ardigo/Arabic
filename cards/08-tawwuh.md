# 8. توّه (tawwuh) — "just (now)"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | توّه |
| Transliteration | tawwuh |
| English | just (did something a moment ago) |
| Forvo | [توه](https://forvo.com/word/توه/#ar) |
| Local audio | [tawwuh.mp4](../audio/tawwuh.mp4) |

Inflects for subject — the citation form is third-person masculine:
`tawwni` = I just, `tawwak` = you (m.) just, `tawwik` = you (f.) just, `tawwuh` = he just, `tawwha` = she just, `tawwna` = we just.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** توّه وصل
**Transliteration:** tawwuh wasal
**English:** He just arrived.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| توّه | tawwuh | just (he) |
| وصل | wasal | arrived |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** توّني جيت
**Transliteration:** tawwni jeet
**English:** I just came.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| توّني | tawwni | just (I) |
| جيت | jeet | I-came |
