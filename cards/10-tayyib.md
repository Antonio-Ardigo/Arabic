# 10. طيب (tayyib) — "okay / nice"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | طيب |
| Transliteration | tayyib |
| English | okay (discourse) / nice (of a person) |
| Forvo | [طيب](https://forvo.com/word/طيب/#ar) |
| Local audio | [tayyib.mp4](../audio/tayyib.mp4) |

Two common uses: (1) conversational "okay, alright" (like English "right, so..."), (2) adjective meaning "nice, kind, good-natured" about a person.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** طيب، نشوفك بكرة
**Transliteration:** tayyib, nshoofak bukra
**English:** Okay, we'll see you tomorrow.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| طيب | tayyib | okay |
| نشوفك | nshoofak | we-see-you (m.) |
| بكرة | bukra | tomorrow |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** هو طيب مره
**Transliteration:** huwa tayyib marra
**English:** He's really nice.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| هو | huwa | he |
| طيب | tayyib | nice |
| مره | marra | very |
