# 11. مره (marra) — "very / a lot"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | مره |
| Transliteration | marra |
| English | very / really (Najdi intensifier) |
| Forvo | [مره](https://forvo.com/word/مره/#ar) |
| Local audio | [marra.mp4](../audio/marra.mp4) |

Najdi intensifier — always placed **after** the adjective (`zain marra`, not `marra zain`). Same spelling as the word for "a time / once" (`marra waahda`), but stress and position disambiguate.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** الجو حار مره
**Transliteration:** al-jaww haar marra
**English:** The weather is really hot.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| الجو | al-jaww | the-weather |
| حار | haar | hot |
| مره | marra | very |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** زين مره
**Transliteration:** zain marra
**English:** Very good.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| زين | zain | good |
| مره | marra | very |
