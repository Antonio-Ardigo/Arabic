# 13. أكيد (akeed) — "definitely / for sure"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | أكيد |
| Transliteration | akeed |
| English | definitely / for sure / certainly |
| Forvo | [أكيد](https://forvo.com/word/أكيد/#ar) |
| Local audio | [akeed.mp4](../audio/akeed.mp4) |

All-purpose confirmation. On its own answers "yes, definitely"; in front of a verb it reinforces it.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** أكيد بجي
**Transliteration:** akeed baji
**English:** I'll definitely come.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| أكيد | akeed | definitely |
| بجي | baji | I-will-come |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** أكيد زين
**Transliteration:** akeed zain
**English:** Definitely good.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| أكيد | akeed | definitely |
| زين | zain | good |
