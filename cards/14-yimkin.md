# 14. يمكن (yimkin) — "maybe"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | يمكن |
| Transliteration | yimkin |
| English | maybe / perhaps |
| Forvo | [يمكن](https://forvo.com/word/يمكن/#ar) |
| Local audio | [yimkin.mp4](../audio/yimkin.mp4) |

Literally "it is possible". Works as a sentence-initial adverb or as a one-word reply.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** يمكن يجي بكرة
**Transliteration:** yimkin yiji bukra
**English:** Maybe he'll come tomorrow.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| يمكن | yimkin | maybe |
| يجي | yiji | he-comes |
| بكرة | bukra | tomorrow |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** يمكن، ما أدري
**Transliteration:** yimkin, ma adri
**English:** Maybe, I don't know.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| يمكن | yimkin | maybe |
| ما | ma | not |
| أدري | adri | I-know |
