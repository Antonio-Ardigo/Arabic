# 15. ودّي (widdi) — "I'd like to / I want to"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | ودّي |
| Transliteration | widdi |
| English | I'd like to / I want to (Najdi) |
| Forvo | [ودي](https://forvo.com/word/ودي/#ar) |
| Local audio | [widdi.mp4](../audio/widdi.mp4) |

Softer than `abgha` — more "I wish / I feel like". Inflects: `widdi` (I), `widdak` (you m.), `widdik` (you f.), `widduh` (he), `widdha` (she). Followed by a verb in the present (no particle between).

## Example 1
**Arabic:** ودّي أنام
**Transliteration:** widdi anaam
**English:** I want to sleep.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| ودّي | widdi | I-want-to |
| أنام | anaam | I-sleep |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** ودّي أشوفك
**Transliteration:** widdi ashoofak
**English:** I want to see you.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| ودّي | widdi | I-want-to |
| أشوفك | ashoofak | I-see-you (m.) |
