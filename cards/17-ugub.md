# 17. عقب (ugub) — "after"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | عقب |
| Transliteration | ugub |
| English | after / afterwards (Najdi; MSA is بعد) |
| Forvo | [عقب](https://forvo.com/word/عقب/#ar) |
| Local audio | [ugub.mp4](../audio/ugub.mp4) |

Najdi preposition for temporal "after". Followed by a noun (`ugub al-ghada` = after lunch) or used on its own as an adverb ("later").

## Example 1
**Arabic:** عقب الدوام
**Transliteration:** ugub ad-dawaam
**English:** After work.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| عقب | ugub | after |
| الدوام | ad-dawaam | the-work-hours |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** نشوفك عقب
**Transliteration:** nshoofak ugub
**English:** We'll see you later.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| نشوفك | nshoofak | we-see-you (m.) |
| عقب | ugub | afterwards |
