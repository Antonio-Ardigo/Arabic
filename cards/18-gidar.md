# 18. قدر (gidar) — "he was able / could"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | قدر |
| Transliteration | gidar |
| English | he was able / could |
| Forvo | [قدر](https://forvo.com/word/قدر/#ar) |
| Local audio | [gidar.mp4](../audio/gidar.mp4) |

Classic example of Najdi ق → `g`. Conjugates: `gidart` (I/you m.), `gidarti` (you f.), `gidar` (he), `gidrat` (she), `gidarna` (we). Followed by a present-tense verb: `gidar yiji` = "he was able to come".

## Example 1
**Arabic:** ما قدر يجي
**Transliteration:** ma gidar yiji
**English:** He couldn't come.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| ما | ma | not |
| قدر | gidar | was-able (he) |
| يجي | yiji | he-comes |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** قدرت أسوّيها
**Transliteration:** gidart asawwiha
**English:** I was able to do it.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| قدرت | gidart | I-was-able |
| أسوّيها | asawwiha | I-do-it (f.) |
