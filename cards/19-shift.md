# 19. شفت (shift) — "I saw / you saw"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | شفت |
| Transliteration | shift |
| English | I saw / you saw (m.) (Najdi; MSA is رأيت) |
| Forvo | [شفت](https://forvo.com/word/شفت/#ar) |
| Local audio | [shift.mp4](../audio/shift.mp4) |

Past-tense stem `shaaf` (to see). `shift` covers 1st-person singular and 2nd-person masculine singular — context picks one. Related forms: `shifti` (you f.), `shaaf` (he), `shaafat` (she), `shifna` (we), `shaafu` (they).

## Example 1
**Arabic:** شفتك أمس
**Transliteration:** shiftak ams
**English:** I saw you yesterday.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| شفتك | shiftak | I-saw-you (m.) |
| أمس | ams | yesterday |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** وش شفت؟
**Transliteration:** wesh shift?
**English:** What did you see?

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| وش | wesh | what |
| شفت | shift | you-saw (m.) |
