# 20. يالله (yalla) — "let's go / come on"

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Arabic | يالله |
| Transliteration | yalla |
| English | let's go / come on / hurry |
| Forvo | [يالله](https://forvo.com/word/يالله/#ar) |
| Local audio | [yalla.mp4](../audio/yalla.mp4) |

From يا الله (literally "O God"), now a pure discourse particle for urging, inviting, or wrapping up. Used constantly in speech.

## Example 1
**Arabic:** يالله، نروح
**Transliteration:** yalla, nruh
**English:** Come on, let's go.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| يالله | yalla | let's-go |
| نروح | nruh | we-go |

## Example 2
**Arabic:** يالله بسرعة
**Transliteration:** yalla b-sur'a
**English:** Hurry up.

| Arabic | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| يالله | yalla | come-on |
| بسرعة | b-sur'a | with-speed |
